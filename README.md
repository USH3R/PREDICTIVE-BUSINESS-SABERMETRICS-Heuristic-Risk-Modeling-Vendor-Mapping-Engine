**Securing Legacy Hardware Foundations: Technical Executive Summary**  
**(With Citations)**  

Legacy hardware in critical systems poses cybersecurity and operational risks due to aging, unsupported devices, limited monitoring, and compatibility issues. Mitigation includes inventorying assets, network segmentation, virtual patching, hardening, and phased replacement, ensuring resilience, compliance, and protection of critical infrastructure.
